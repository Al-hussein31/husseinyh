About me

I'm Hussein — a full-stack developer and founder based in Nigeria. I started writing code because I wanted to build things. I started a company because I wanted those things to matter.

At ForgeGrowth, I build the system that turns Nigerian businesses' WhatsApp into a 24/7 sales machine — capturing leads, replying instantly with AI, following up automatically, and closing customers while the owner sleeps. I wear every hat: architecture, TypeScript, client systems, and business strategy at 2am.

I also build in the open. Lodenix gives AI tools real-world signal via a single API call. NaijaMind is a fine-tuned LLM that finally understands Nigerian Pidgin, business context, and culture. Both Apache 2.0 — built to be used, not gatekept.

I work in NeoVim + Ghostty because your tools shape how you think. Deep in TypeScript daily, picking up Go for systems work, learning Rust because some problems deserve the right tool.

// The business

Forge Growth

ForgeGrowth is a WhatsApp Growth System for Nigerian businesses. Not a website agency. We build and manage the system that turns a business's WhatsApp into a 24/7 sales machine — capturing leads, replying instantly, following up automatically, and closing customers while the owner focuses on running their business.

Nigeria has 52 million+ WhatsApp Business downloads. 95% of Nigeria's digital population uses WhatsApp. Yet most Nigerian SMBs manage it manually — losing leads to slow replies, forgotten follow-ups, and zero automation. We fix the system. One closed deal from WhatsApp covers the entire monthly fee.

NaijaMind

An AI that actually speaks Nigerian. Fine-tuned on Qwen 3 to understand Pidgin, local business context, and how people here really communicate.
